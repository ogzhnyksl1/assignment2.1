Top Albums

  1. Master of Puppets (1986)

    Considered one of the greatest metal albums of all time, "Master of Puppets" features powerful riffs, intricate song structures, and iconic tracks like "Battery" and the title track.

  2. The Black Album (1991)

    This self-titled album, often referred to as "The Black Album," marked a shift towards a more mainstream sound. It includes hits like "Enter Sandman" and "Nothing Else Matters."

  3. Death Magnetic (2008)

    "Death Magnetic" showcases Metallica's return to a heavier sound. The album received critical acclaim for its energetic performances and intricate compositions.
